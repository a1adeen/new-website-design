Daisy

The daisy is a common flower that belongs to the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe, but it can be found in many parts of the world today. The daisy has a yellow center that is surrounded by white petals, although there are also varieties with pink, red, or purple petals. The daisy is often associated with innocence and purity, and it has been used in many cultures for medicinal purposes. The daisy is also a popular ornamental flower, and it can be found in gardens and wildflower meadows.
