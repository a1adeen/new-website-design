lavender

Lavender is a flowering plant that belongs to the mint family and is native to the Mediterranean region. It has narrow, gray-green leaves and fragrant purple or blue flowers. Daisy

The daisy is a common flower that belongs to the Asteraceae family. It is native to Europe, but it can be found in many parts of the world today. The daisy has a yellow center that is surrounded by white petals, although there are also varieties with pink, red, or purple petals. more about it
